# the rhythm
![the rhythm](images/the%20rhythm.jpeg)

can you hear it?
`i don’t hear it`
listen closely
`i still don’t hear it`
listen with your eyes.
`the trees?`
look closer
`the birds? the bees? what is it?`
lean back
`is it the forest? the sun? the stars?`
look into the water
`i hear it now! `
keep listening :)
